About the software

SmartERP is a complete back-office solution for education groups — manage schools under your sanstha, daily accounts, receipts & payments, staff and teacher records, documents, inventory, events, and reports from a single secure application.

संस्था व शाळांचे प्रशासन, लेखा, कर्मचारी/शिक्षक माहिती, कागदपत्रे, वाउचर व अहवाल एकाच ठिकाणी व्यवस्थापित करण्यासाठी ही प्रणाली विकसित केली आहे.

Key modules

  • Accounts & Vouchers

    Receipt, payment, bank deposit, ledger and account registers

  • School & Organization

    School master, sanstha setup, organization documents

  • Staff & Teachers

    Employee and teacher profiles, documents, school history

  • Masters & Reports

    Document, category, subject masters and financial reports
